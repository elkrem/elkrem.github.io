Features

Simple SDK

Our SDK is Arduino compatible and requires no setup and no prior knowledge in decentralization or how it works.

Blockchain Agnostic

While our SDK ships with Ethereum and Bitcoin support, Elk is designed from the ground up to be blockchain agnostic and support for more platforms will continually be added.

Truly Decentralized

By default and when possible, Elk runs a light node and communicates directly with the blockchain and not through a hosted service. Elk pushes decentralization to the edge!

Open Source

Our hardware and SDK will both be 100% open-source. Hack away!

Breadboard Compatible

We want to make your hard hardware life easy. Easily connect Elk with your sensors and actuators using your breadboard.
