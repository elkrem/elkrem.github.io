Our Mission

We are on a mission to introduce the world to "Decent IoT". "Decent IoT" is decentralized, gives users true control and true privacy, and allows entirely new use-cases like payments, oracles, selling your data, and much more.

We believe the potential for blockchain and decentralization extends far beyond the hype, and that decentralization allows us to build IoT that is far more private, far more secure, and far more capable.

If you're a blockchain enthusiast or a privacy-conscious maker, give us a shout and let us know your thoughts!
